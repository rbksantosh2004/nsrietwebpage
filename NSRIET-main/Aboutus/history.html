History

Nadimpalli Satyanarayana Raju Institute of Technology (Formerly known as Vizag Institute of Technology) sponsored by Oxford Educational Society was established in the year 2008 at Visakhapatnam, AndhraPradesh with an Intake of 240. Currently the Intake has grown to 708 with in a span of seven years. All the courses offered by the college are approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The College is affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK). The Polytechnic courses are affiliated to State Board of Technical Education and Training, AP (SBTET).
